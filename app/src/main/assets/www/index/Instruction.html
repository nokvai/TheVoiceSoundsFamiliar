Game Instruction

What’s is this game?

• The player can ENTER its name and gender and MANAGE settings (sound and theme).

• Adjust sound VOLUME, select player and CHANGE THEME for the game.

• Songs questions in this game are RANDOMLY GENERATED.

• Every end of the level will display a REAL TIME LEADER BOARD to show the players highest score.

• Level of difficulties: the BEGINNER, ADVANCED, and EXPERT.

• Beginner is the first level, a player has given an allotted 50 POINTS before starting the game.

• In the BEGINNER LEVEL, answering questions will be in MULTIPLE CHOICE formats.

• A player should EARN 80 POINTS before proceeding to ADVANCED LEVEL.

• Advanced is the SECOND LEVEL, answering questions will be in FILL IN THE BLANK format.

• A player should EARN 100 POINTS before proceeding to the last level which is EXPERT.

• EXPERT LEVEL will be GUESSING THE TITLE.

• Every level has a 50 SONGS but have a 30 QUESTIONS are available to be answered.

• There is a corresponding HINTS in every QUESTIONS per LEVEL.

• Every use of hints there will be a corresponding DEDUCTION worth of 40 POINTS.

• Every answered question there will be a corresponding 5 POINTS that will add to their allotted points.

• A PLAYER DIDN’T KNOW IF THE ANSWER IS CORRECT during playing the game, otherwise he/she can KNOW THE NUMBERS OF CORRECT ANSWER EVERY END OF LEVEl.
